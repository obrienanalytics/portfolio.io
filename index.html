England Housing Market (1995-2016)

This Power BI dashboard provides an in-depth financial analysis of home prices across England from 1995 to 2016. Leveraging DAX and Power Query (M), it highlights pricing trends across six major regions using interactive charts, drilldowns, and drillthroughs. Each report page supports cross-filtering and cross-highlighting, enabling users to explore the data from multiple perspectives and uncover regional and historical insights. Ideal for professionals in real estate, finance, and market forecasting.
